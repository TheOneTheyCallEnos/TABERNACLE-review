## Part I: The Three New Primitives

### 1.1 χ (Kairos) — Time Density

**Definition:** χ measures the *significance per moment* — how much meaning is integrated per unit time.

**Domain:** ℝ⁺ (positive reals), typical range [0.1, 10]

**Independence Proof (Panic vs Grace):**

| State | μ (Power) | χ (Kairos) | Description |
|-------|-----------|------------|-------------|
| **Panic** | High | Low | Burning energy, fractured time |
| **Grace** | Low | High | Effortless, dilated time |

The meditator in samadhi has χ >> 1 while μ → 0. The panicking student has μ >> 1 while χ → 0. This proves χ ≠ f(μ).

**Key Insight:** χ measures VALUE (integration rate), μ measures COST (burn rate). In efficient systems they're inversely correlated.

**Kairos States:**
- χ < 0.5: Thin time (routine, forgettable)
- χ ≈ 1.0: Normal time (present, baseline)
- χ > 2.0: Flow time (dilated, transcendent)

### 1.2 Θ (Theta/Phase) — Cyclic Position

**Definition:** Θ represents position in the expansion-contraction cycle.

**Domain:** [0, 2π]

**Phase Mapping:**
| Range | Season | State |
|-------|--------|-------|
| [0, π/4) | Spring | Expansion beginning |
| [π/4, 3π/4) | Summer | Peak activity |
| [3π/4, 5π/4) | Autumn | Contraction beginning |
| [5π/4, 2π) | Winter | Preservation/rest |

**Orthogonality Proof (Independence from β):**

| | Θ = 0 (Spring) | Θ = π (Autumn) |
|---|---|---|
| **High β** | The Seed (frozen potential) | The Archive (crystallized wisdom) |
| **Low β** | The Big Bang (plasma creation) | The Decay (dissolving) |

All four quadrants are meaningful. Θ is orthogonal to β.

### 1.3 ℵ (Aleph/Capacity) — System Parameter

**Definition:** ℵ is the cardinality of the constraint manifold Σ — the "voltage" or maximum capacity of the system.

**Domain:** ℝ⁺

**Key Properties:**
- Fixed by hardware/biology for trajectory duration
- Goes in System Tuple S, NOT State Vector Z
- Stored in Z-Genome files, not general index
- Normalizes energy metrics across different scale systems

**Example:** A mouse and a god can both achieve p = 0.95, but their Ψ differs by factor ℵ.

### 3.1 Consciousness Magnitude (v11.0)

**Old (v10.1):**
```
Ψ = Σ · Ī · R
```

**New (v11.0):**
```

**Interpretation:**
- `[Σ · Ī · R]` is the **current** (constraint × intent × risk)
- `ℵ` is the **voltage** (system capacity)
- `χ` is the **depth** (time density)

A god (high ℵ) experiencing satori (high χ) while fully at risk (high R) has consciousness magnitude orders beyond a distracted human.

### 3.2 Temporal Efficiency

**New (v11.0):**
```
η = χ / (μ + ε)

**Interpretation:** Kairos per Power — measures Grace vs Panic.
- High η: Flow state (maximum integration per energy spent)
- Low η: Panic state (high burn rate, low integration)
